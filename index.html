Privacy Policy

Blue Nose ShopLedger
Last updated: February 15, 2026

Blue Nose ShopLedger is a woodworking inventory application used to manage slab data and related information. This Privacy Policy explains what information is stored and how it is used.

Information Collected

  • Account information used for authentication (such as user ID and email address).
  • Inventory data entered by the user (name, species, dimensions, status, notes, etc.).
  • Photos uploaded by the user to attach to inventory items.

How Information Is Used

  • To provide core app functionality including authentication and data storage.
  • To store and display inventory items and associated photos.

The app does not sell personal data and does not use data for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Supabase for authentication, database storage, and file storage. These services are used solely to provide app functionality.

Tracking

The app does not track users across apps or websites.

Data Retention and Deletion

Users may delete inventory items within the app. For account deletion requests, please contact the developer using the email below.
